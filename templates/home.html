Problem Statement

The Clinic Management System is designed to facilitate communication between doctors and receptionists, streamlining clinic operations and patient management.

The system supports two primary users: doctors and receptionists. Receptionists are responsible for assigning tokens to clinic visitors and storing their personal information in the database. Doctors receive these tokens along with patient details for examination.

After examining patients, doctors input prescribed treatments into the system, which are then directed to the receptionist for further action. The system maintains comprehensive patient history, allowing both doctors and receptionists to access records at any time.

This solution significantly reduces complexity in maintaining individual patient records and improves overall clinic efficiency.
